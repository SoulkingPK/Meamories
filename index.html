Our Hidden World

Just the two of us 🌙
Favourite memories ✨
Miss u celebrity ✨

Enaku enga start pandrathune theriyala… but nee dhaan eppovume en comfort zone. Un kooda irukka every memory-um — adhu meaningful moment ah irundhalum illa namma pannuna random stupid things ah irundhalum — enaku romba special. Un kitta dhaan naan full childish-ah, annoying-ah, loosu madhiri behave pannen… aana nee oru naal kooda adha thappa eduthukala. Nee calm-ah irundha, enna listen panna, enaku eppovume support-ah irundha no matter what. Nee scold panna moments-um, adicha moments-um kooda care nala dhaan nu enaku theriyum… honestly adhuvum enaku thevai dhan. Naan edhavadhu kashtathula irundha, proper-ah explain panna kooda mudiyama irundhalum nee enna purinjukitta. Nee kudutha support ah words-la explain panna mudiyadhu. Enaku nee just best friend illa… nee enaku romba special person, literally en favorite person. Unna miss panradhu illa, unna lose panradhu nenacha kooda romba kashtama iruku… because nee en life-la avlo important ah aayita. Nee eppovume ipdiye en kooda irukanum, support pannikanum nu dhaan aasai… because honestly, nee illa na life adhe madhiri feel aagadhu. 💛

— only for you ♥
